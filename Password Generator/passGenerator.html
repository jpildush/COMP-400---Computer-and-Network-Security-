Password Generator

---By Joseph Pildush



Password Criteria:
  • Password must be between 8 and 16 characters in length
  • A strong password will contain at least 1 number
  • A strong password will contain at least 1 uppercase and 1 lowercase letter
  • A strong password will contain more than 2 special characters
  • The best password would not have too many duplicate characters




Generator has been designed in such a way that there will not be a special character after another special character, nor a letter after another letter.
